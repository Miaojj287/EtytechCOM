OUR STORY

In 2022, we started the ETYTECH to disrupt the current state of the development industry, which is often burdened by high entry barriers, non-transparent pricing, and outdated solutions, and by leveraging the power of code, we empower businesses across industries to improve efficiency and deliver better services to their customers. Our two founders have a clear division of roles: Ethan focuses on product marketing and client engagement, having earned a Master's degree in Technology Management, while Eric, who is currently pursuing a PhD in Artificial Intelligence, leads the technical team in executing development projects with innovation and precision.

We believe that every company—and even every individual—should have the opportunity to enjoy the benefits of technology, and this vision drives our continuous effort to make advanced solutions more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable.
